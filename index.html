About Me

Hello! My name is Long Huynh and I am 25 years old. I am a Vietnamese refugee and came to America when I was 5 years old. I am very thankful for this country and the oppurtunities it gave for my family.I am a graduate from the University Of Georgia. I majored in Biology and worked as an Emergency Medical Technician after college. I enjoyed the time I have spent in the medical field as it gave me a greater appreciation for our healthcare workers. However, it did not feel as what I wanted to do for my life. Eventually, I decided to pursue a career in the IT field as a fullstack/software developer. I am loving the journey so far. In my spare time I am a Youth Group Leader at my church, I am very passionate about working with the youth and guiding them to become good citizens. My hobbies include volleyball and video games!
